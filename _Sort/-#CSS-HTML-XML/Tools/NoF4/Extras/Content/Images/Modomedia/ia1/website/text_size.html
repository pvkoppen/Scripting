Text Size


The size of the text you choose for your buttons and headers will have a big effect on the readability of your images. In this example, the first image uses 8pt text. (the font is Switzerland Condensed Bold)

By reducing the word "Information" to simply "Info", we can increase the text size to 12 point. Look at the difference this makes in our image.

Here is the image again with 14 point text. In many cases the viewer will prefer to have fewer words, and a more legible interface.
