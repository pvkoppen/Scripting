Technical Support information for NetObjects Fusion:

The following forms of support are available to you:

  • WebSupport. As a registered customer you receive 90 days of free, unlimited access to NetObjects WebSupport . Getting help is now as close as your nearest browser. You can create support cases, check the status of existing cases, and research old cases. All support cases will be handled by our support staff within one business day.
     
  •  Newsgroups. Our active newsgroups are filled with answers, tips, analysis, and comments from your fellow site designers and developers.
     
  •  Telephone. You can purchase premium telephone support from 8am to 5pm Pacific Time, Monday through Friday. Simply call and have your NetObjects Technical Support PIN number ready. Inside the U.S./Canada, call 1-888-888-8993. Outside the U.S./Canada, contact your local reseller or distributor – please see the NetObjects Web site for listings or call 1-650-482-5214.
